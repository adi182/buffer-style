Posts

Posts are templates that display content added to Buffer and live within different parts of the Buffer app.

Post within Content → Queue

Use this template to communicate a regular post to the user.

Example:
What is a Product Designer? An awesome story by @jgadapee over on Medium! http://buff.ly/1LTbUqv
09:42 (GMT)
via Extension
