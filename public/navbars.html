NavBar

The NavBar component displays navigational objects.

Example:
Awesome plan
  • Content
  • Analytics
  • Schedule
  • Settings
  • Help
  • Account

  </div>
  <ul class="NavBar__LeftMenu">
    <li class="NavBar__Menu__item"><a class="NavBar__Menu__item--active" href="#">Content</a></li>
    <li class="NavBar__Menu__item"><a href="#">Analytics</a></li>
    <li class="NavBar__Menu__item"><a href="#">Schedule</a></li>

  </ul>
</div>
