Modals

Modals are templates that have a singular responsibility and are designed by nature to focus the user's attention.

Modal

Use this template as a base to build other modals.

Example:
Open Modal

Modal content.

<div class="code-block-render">
  <a href="#openModal">Open Modal</a>
  <div class="modal" id="openModal">
    <div class="card card--no-border">
      <div class="modal-content">
        <p>Modal content.</p>
        <a class="modal-exit" href="#close" title="Close"><i class="bi bi-x"></i></a>
      </div>
    </div>
  </div>
</div>
