Static Pages

Static Pages are built up of blocks. Each block has different variations and each variation can have a singular modifier. A singular modifier is a modifier that changes a single attribute of the entire block.

Hero Block with title and subtitle

This block-hero includes a title and a subtitle. Its modifier changes the background image. The example below uses the pricing page modifier, block-hero--pricing.

Example:

Find Your Perfect Plan

Enjoy the voice you deserve on social media with our Buffer social publishing tools, free for as long as you'd like.

<div class="block-hero block-hero--pricing">
  <h1>Find Your Perfect Plan</h1>
  <h2>Enjoy the voice you deserve on social media with our Buffer social publishing tools, free for as long as you'd like.</h2>
</div>
